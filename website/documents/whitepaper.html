Executive Overview

MissionChain is a decentralized learning and contribution protocol built on Binance Smart Chain (BSC). It introduces a tokenized knowledge economy where participants earn, stake, and govern through verified on-chain activity.

Unlike speculative DeFi models, MissionChain anchors token value to measurable human output: completed lessons, peer reviews, mission contributions, and community governance participation.

Core Mission: Create a self-sustaining ecosystem where education, contribution, and token economics reinforce each other — building real value through real human activity.

No Fixed TGE Policy

MissionChain deliberately avoids a fixed Token Generation Event. Token release follows the Hindex emission curve, tying new supply directly to ecosystem activity. This eliminates artificial price shocks common in scheduled TGE launches.

03

Hindex Algorithm

The Hindex (Human-index) is MissionChain's core emission engine. Inspired by the academic h-index, it measures sustained contribution quality — not just quantity.

Emission Formula

ADAPTIVE EMISSION ENGINE — MASTER FORMULA
E(t) = E_base(t) × D(t) × R(t)
VariableNameDescription
E(t)Daily EmissionTotal MIC minted per day, split 60/15/20/5
E_base(t)Base EmissionE₀ × 2^(−t/T_half) — exponential decay, T_half = 180 days, E₀ ≈ 22.9M MIC/day
D(t)Demand Factor0.5 + U(t), where U = activeMICE / 100,000. Range [0.5, 1.5]
R(t)ROI Regulatorclamp(ROI_target / ROI_current, 0.5, 2.0) — targets 250% annual miner ROI

Emission split: 60% Miners (Hindex-weighted) | 15% DAO Treasury | 20% NFT Stakers | 5% Auto-Burn. All ratios adjustable by DAO vote within guardrails.

Hindex Score Calculation

H-SCORE FORMULA
H(t) = √( Σ Cᵢ × Qᵢ × Rᵢ ) / N_active

Where Cᵢ = contribution count, Qᵢ = quality score (peer-verified), Rᵢ = recency weight, N_active = active participant count.

MICE License Framework

MICE (Mission Income Chain Engine) licenses are time-limited emission amplifiers. Holders earn MIC proportional to their Hindex contributions during the license validity period.

ParameterValue
License PriceDynamic $300 – $1,000 USDT (based on utilization)
License Duration360 days
Emission Pool85% unified mining pool (5.95B MIC) — mined via MICE
TransferabilityPeer-to-peer via Marketplace
RenewalPurchasable at any time
Maximum per walletUnlimited

MICE licenses do not represent equity, dividends, or guaranteed returns. Emission depends entirely on Hindex activity. Inactive wallets receive zero emission regardless of license count.

NFT Tier System

06

DAO Governance

MissionChain transitions to full DAO governance upon reaching Phase III milestones. Token holders vote on protocol parameters, treasury allocation, and ecosystem partnerships.

Governance Rights

  • Adjust emission parameters (split ratios, ROI target, T_half, MICE pricing) within hard-coded guardrails, max ±5% per quarter
  • Approve ecosystem reserve disbursements above 10M MIC
  • Ratify new MICE license structures and pricing
  • Elect protocol stewards and technical committee members
  • Vote on strategic partnerships and exchange listings

Voting Power

VOTING WEIGHT
V = MIC_staked × T_factor × NFT_multiplier

Where T_factor = time-weighted average staking duration (max 2×), NFT_multiplier = tier multiplier of highest-tier NFT held.

08

Ecosystem Architecture

The MissionChain ecosystem consists of three core applications sharing the MIC token layer:

  • Mission Learn — Education platform with verified credentials. Completion triggers Hindex score updates and NFT eligibility.
  • Mission Work — Decentralized task marketplace. Contributors earn MIC for verified deliverables.
  • Mission Social — Reputation-weighted social graph. Organic growth amplifies Hindex scores.

All three apps are non-custodial. Users retain full control of their wallets, credentials, and earned tokens at all times.

Security & Audit

All MissionChain smart contracts undergo independent third-party audits prior to mainnet deployment. Audit reports are published on-chain and available at docs.missionchain.info.

  • Multi-sig treasury (5-of-9 Gnosis Safe)
  • Time-lock on all governance-controlled parameters (72-hour minimum)
  • Emergency pause mechanism controlled by 3-of-5 security council
  • Bug bounty program active from Phase I launch
