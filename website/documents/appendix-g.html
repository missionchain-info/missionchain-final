G.1

NIRA — AI Assistant Overview

NIRA (Neural Intelligence for Rapid Assistance) is MissionChain's embedded AI assistant powered by Anthropic's Claude API. NIRA provides protocol information, onboarding guidance, and real-time support within the MissionChain DApp.

NIRA operates as an informational assistant only. It cannot execute transactions, access wallets, or modify user accounts. All financial decisions remain solely with the user.

G.3

Data Privacy

NIRA conversations are processed by Anthropic's Claude API. MissionChain does not store conversation history permanently. The following data handling policies apply:

  • Conversation content is transmitted to Anthropic's API and subject to Anthropic's privacy policy
  • No wallet addresses or private keys should ever be shared with NIRA
  • Session data is cleared upon logout or tab close
  • Aggregate (anonymized) usage statistics may be retained for product improvement

Best practice: Never share seed phrases, private keys, or passwords with NIRA or any AI assistant under any circumstances.

G.4

AI Governance Framework

The integration of AI in MissionChain governance processes follows a strict human-in-the-loop model:

  • AI may not initiate governance proposals, votes, or treasury actions
  • AI may assist in drafting proposal summaries and impact analysis
  • All binding actions require cryptographic wallet signature from authorized human participants
  • AI-generated content used in official communications must be disclosed

Future AI Integrations (Roadmap)

  • Automated Hindex fraud detection (Phase II)
  • AI-powered KYC anomaly detection (Phase II)
  • Governance proposal quality scoring (Phase III)
  • Personalized learning path recommendations — Mission Learn (Phase III)
G.5

AI Accountability

MissionChain maintains an AI oversight committee responsible for reviewing AI system behavior, auditing outputs for accuracy, and updating system prompts in response to user feedback. NIRA's knowledge base is updated with each protocol upgrade cycle (approximately quarterly).

Users who identify incorrect information provided by NIRA are encouraged to report it via the in-app feedback mechanism. Confirmed errors are corrected within 72 hours of report.
