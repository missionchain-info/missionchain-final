A.3

Vesting Schedule

SEED investors are subject to a structured vesting schedule designed to align long-term incentives and prevent early market pressure:

  • Initial Unlock: 10% unlocked after 6 months from purchase
  • Linear Release: 2.5% per month unlocked after initial cliff
  • MICE Licenses: Active immediately upon purchase confirmation
  • Bonus MIC: Same vesting schedule as principal allocation
  • Full Vesting: 100% vested at month 42 from purchase

SEED participants access MICE license benefits from Day 1, allowing immediate participation in the Hindex emission pool regardless of token vesting status.

A.4

Eligibility & KYC

All SEED round participants must complete KYC/AML verification. Restricted jurisdictions include: United States, Iran, North Korea, and other OFAC-listed territories. Verification is processed via third-party provider with results within 48 hours.

Accepted Payment Methods

  • USDT (BEP-20 on BSC)
  • BNB (converted at PancakeSwap V3 TWAP rate)
