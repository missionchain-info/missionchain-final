E.1

Economic Design Philosophy

MissionChain's token economy is designed around three principles: Activity-anchored value, anti-inflationary emission, and community-owned growth. No single actor can capture disproportionate value without proportional ecosystem contribution.

Master Emission Formula

ADAPTIVE EMISSION ENGINE
E(t) = E_base(t) × D(t) × R(t)

Where E_base(t) = E₀ × 2^(−t/T_half), T_half = 180 days, E₀ ≈ 22.9M MIC/day. D(t) = 0.5 + U(t) — MICE demand factor [0.5, 1.5]. R(t) = clamp(250% / ROI_current, 0.5, 2.0) — ROI regulator targeting 250% annual miner ROI.

All split ratios are DAO-adjustable within guardrails: miners [50-70%], DAO [10-20%], staking [15-25%], burn [3-10%]. Max change ±5% per quarter. Sum must equal 100%.

E.3

Deflationary Mechanisms

  • Auto-Burn (5% emission): 5% of all mined MIC is permanently burned (~297.5M MIC over lifecycle)
  • MICE Revenue Buyback & Burn: 20% of MICE license revenue used for MIC buyback and burn (~240M MIC estimated)
  • Governance Burn: Failed proposals burn 500 MIC submission fee
  • Inactivity Reclaim: Unclaimed emissions after 180 days return to protocol reserve

Total deflationary impact estimated at ~537.5M MIC (7.7% of total supply) combining auto-burn and buyback mechanisms. Note: MICE is ERC-1155 NFT — MICE licenses are NOT burned, only MIC tokens.

E.5

Liquidity & Market Making

1.50% of total supply (105M MIC) is allocated for DEX/CEX listing at estimated price $0.01. Liquidity is managed by a Gnosis Safe 3-of-5 multisig with 48-hour time-lock on significant withdrawals. Initial DEX listing targets PancakeSwap V3 concentrated liquidity pools. Additional liquidity funded by 30% of MICE license revenue directed to MIC/USDT liquidity pool.
