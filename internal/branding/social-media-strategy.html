Section B

MIC Airdrop Strategy for the Christian Community

The MIC airdrop is Mission Chain's most powerful community acquisition tool. The design must be accessible to non-crypto users — most Christians in the Global South have never used a Web3 wallet.

💡 Core Design Principle: "Earn by Serving, Not by Speculating"

Every airdrop task must align with Mission Chain's values. Tasks are called "Missions" — small acts of faith, creativity, and community building that reward participants with MIC. This is not a traditional airdrop; it's an activation event.

🚀 Non-Crypto User Onboarding Flow

  1. Join via familiar channel — Telegram link, Facebook group, church WhatsApp. No wallet required at first.
  2. Register with email + phone — Mission Chain creates a custodial wallet automatically. User sees "MIC Balance" not technical addresses.
  3. Complete Welcome Mission — Simple task (watch intro video, answer 3 questions about faith). Earn 100 MIC instantly.
  4. Guided wallet upgrade — After earning, prompted to set up MetaMask / Trust Wallet to "own your MIC." Step-by-step tutorial in local language.
  5. Church/Group Referral — Share your referral link in your church group. Both you and each new member earn bonus MIC.

Section D

Christian Influencer & Church Partnership Strategy

The highest-trust pathway into the Christian community is through existing trusted leaders. Mission Chain's partnership approach is servant-first — we bring value to pastors and church networks before asking anything in return.

🤝 Approach: Serve First, Partner Second

The Fatal Mistake of Web3 Projects

Most crypto projects approach churches with "shill our token to your congregation." That destroys trust immediately. Mission Chain's approach is fundamentally different: we solve real problems churches already have — finding skilled volunteer creators, funding ministry media, equipping young people with economic skills — and MIC is the natural reward mechanism within that.

📩

Initial Outreach Script (Pastor/Leader)

  • "We are building a creative economy for Christian talent"
  • "We'd love to feature your church's worship team"
  • "We want to help your young people earn from their gifts"
  • "Zero cost — we provide the platform, you provide the community"
  • Never lead with token investment or price
🎁

  • Free portfolio platform for their worship/creative team
  • MIC airdrop for congregation members (bulk allocation)
  • Certified "Mission Hub" branding and recognition
  • Access to Mission Work marketplace for hiring talent
